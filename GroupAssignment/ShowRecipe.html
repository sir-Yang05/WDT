Grilled Salmon Salad

⏱ Prep: 15 mins 🔥 350 kcal 👥 Serves: 2 (Adjustable)

Ingredients

  • 200g Fresh Salmon
  • 1 cup Mixed Greens
  • 1/2 Avocado
  • Olive oil & Lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Season the salmon: Rub the salmon fillets with a little olive oil, salt, and black pepper.
  2. Grill: Heat a grill pan over medium-high heat. Cook the salmon for 3-4 minutes per side.
  3. Assemble: Toss the mixed greens and sliced avocado with lemon juice, then top with the grilled salmon.
